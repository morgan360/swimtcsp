Public swims encompass a range of activities to suit everyone's needs. Family swims offer a playful environment for all ages, while lane swims provide a focused space for improving techniques and fitness. Early morning swims are perfect for those seeking a peaceful start to their day, and masters swimming challenges the more experienced with advanced training and competition. Aqua aerobics classes combine fun and fitness, making exercise enjoyable with minimal joint strain. This diverse offering ensures that regardless of your age or skill level, there's an aquatic activity to engage and energize you.

Swimming lessons cater to learners of all ages and skill levels, from toddlers taking their first dip to adults perfecting their strokes. For children, lessons are structured around fun and safety, focusing on basic skills like floating, kicking, and gaining confidence in the water. As swimmers progress, instruction shifts towards refining technique, improving endurance, and introducing competitive strokes like freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly. Adult classes offer a supportive environment for beginners to overcome water apprehension and for more seasoned swimmers to enhance their efficiency and speed in the water. Specialized courses, such as private lessons, provide personalized attention to meet specific goals. Through these structured programs, swimming lessons not only promote water safety but also encourage lifelong fitness and enjoyment of swimming.

Our schools program is available for participating schools. Lessons can be booked and paid for through our site.
